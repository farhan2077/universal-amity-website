What do we do?

Helping people is our mantra. If any people need help, we are there to help. Even though we do various types of charity works, we can only focus on a few.

Aid for life

Helping those who are financially weak not only improves their livlihood but also improves the overall socio-economic situation of the society.

Aid for education

Helping financially weak but meritorious students so that they can continue their study which is very important for us.

Food for the unfed

The worst pain in the world in the pain of not being able to afford food. So we always try to make sure this doesn't happen to anyone.
